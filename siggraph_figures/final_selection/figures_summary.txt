XGeoRTR SIGGRAPH figure ranking and final selection

Selected figures

1. xgeort_multiscale.jpg
Short description: Two-panel UMAP embedding showing global explanation geometry and a zoomed local region with sparse contribution vectors.
Why selected: It most clearly communicates the global-versus-local explanation structure needed for the poster while keeping colors and arrows readable.

2. xgeort_representative.jpg
Short description: Representative UMAP embedding with prediction-colored points and sparse contribution-direction arrows.
Why selected: It works as the single hero image for XGeoRTR because it presents backend explanation state, class structure, and local directions in one uncluttered panel.

3. xgeort_attribution.jpg
Short description: UMAP embedding where color shows the dominant normalized SHAP-like feature group and glyph size/intensity shows attribution magnitude.
Why selected: It directly supports the feature-importance-to-space claim with only four feature groups and optional density contours for spatial context.

Not selected

4. xgeort_structure.jpg
Short description: Static transition paths through embedding neighborhoods ordered by prediction contrast.
Why not selected: It is conceptually useful, but the long path summaries are visually heavier than the other assets and are less suitable for the compact poster figure set.
